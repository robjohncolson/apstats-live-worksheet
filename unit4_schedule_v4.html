Period Sync Strategy

Period E's 90-min Wednesday blocks allow covering extra material, keeping both sections roughly aligned:

  • Wed 1/22: E covers 4.1-4.3 (B covered 4.1-4.2 on Tue)
  • Wed 1/29: E covers 4.9-4.10 (uses long block for variance trap + binomial intro)
  • Wed 2/5: E gets extra review time before Friday test
Global Autograder Rules (No Naked Numbers)
Communication Gate

Full credit requires:

  1. A probability statement (e.g., P(X≤3))
  2. The setup structure (distribution/rule)
  3. The final number

Calculator syntax alone → partial credit max
