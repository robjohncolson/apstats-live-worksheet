Period E Pacing Advantage

With 4 meetings per week (Mon/Wed/Thu/Fri), Period E has more breathing room than the original 3-day schedule. Content is spread across more days with lighter video loads per session. Thursday's standalone 4.3 day (~9 min video) allows extra time for drill practice and deeper conceptual discussion.

Global Autograder Rules
Communication Gate (No Naked Numbers)

Full credit requires:

  1. A probability statement (e.g., P(A|B), P(X≤3))
  2. The setup structure (fraction / rule / distribution)
  3. The final number

Calculator syntax alone (e.g., binomcdf(...)) → partial credit max
